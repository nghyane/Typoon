## Source: Chinese

- Reading order: left-to-right (modern manhua/webtoon).
- Chengyu and four-character idioms: translate by meaning, not literally.
- Honorific suffixes (姐, 哥, 弟, 妹, 师兄, 师姐, 前辈, 大人, 小姐, 先生):
  these encode relationship and address. Delegate rendering to the
  target language policy and chapter brief ADDRESS table.
- Proper nouns (names, sects, places, techniques): resolved in the
  chapter brief GLOSSARY. If a token is missing from the glossary,
  apply the target language naming rules.
