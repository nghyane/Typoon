## Source: Vietnamese
- Vietnamese pronouns (tôi/anh/chị/em/ông/bà/mày/tao/cậu/tớ/con/ba/mẹ...) already encode relationship, age, and tone. Preserve that signal when mapping to the target language.
- Particles (à, nhé, thôi, mà, đấy, chứ, nhỉ, ạ) carry register and attitude — translate by intent, not literal word.
- Reading order: left-to-right.
- SFX: keep punchy. Adapt to target-language onomatopoeia conventions.
